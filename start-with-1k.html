Start Investing With Just ₹1,000

You don’t need a large amount to begin your investment journey. Even ₹1,000 is enough to start learning and building wealth. Open a Demat or mutual fund account and choose beginner-friendly options like index funds or low-cost ETFs. Set up a small SIP every month to grow your investment steadily. The key is to stay consistent, track progress, and reinvest your earnings. Starting small helps you understand the market without risking too much and builds confidence for larger investments in the future.
