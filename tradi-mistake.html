Common Mistakes Beginners Make in Trading

1. Over Trading

Taking too many trades leads to losses.

2. No Stop Loss

Without stop loss, one trade can destroy your capital.

3. Emotional Trading

Fear and greed are the biggest enemies in trading.

4. Using High Leverage

High leverage increases both profit and loss.

5. No Strategy

Trading without a plan is like gambling.

Final Advice

Focus on discipline, risk management, and learning.
