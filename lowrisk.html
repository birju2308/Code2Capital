Best Low-Risk Investments for Students

Students can start investing safely by choosing low-risk options that protect capital and provide steady returns. Consider investing in large-cap mutual funds, government bonds, or fixed deposits. SIP in index funds is another excellent choice for long-term growth. Avoid high-risk trading until you gain experience. The goal is to learn the basics, stay disciplined, and grow wealth gradually. Even small, regular investments can compound significantly over time and teach financial responsibility early in life.
