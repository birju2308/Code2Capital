Top 5 Online Side Hustles for Students

Students can earn money online while learning valuable skills. Some popular side hustles include freelancing in programming or design, content writing, tutoring online, starting a small e-commerce store, or participating in affiliate marketing. These opportunities require minimal investment and flexible time management. By dedicating a few hours weekly, students can earn extra income to cover expenses or invest for the future. Combining learning with earning creates a strong financial and skill-building foundation early in life.
